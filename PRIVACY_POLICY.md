# Privacy Policy for Spot the Shade

**Last updated:** March 7, 2026

**Developer:** Krafted Apps
**Package:** app.krafted.spottheshade

## Overview

Spot the Shade is a color-matching puzzle game for Android. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

Spot the Shade does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and makes no network requests.

- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting services
- No advertising SDKs or ad tracking
- No account or login system
- No server communication of any kind

## Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Android DataStore:

- Game scores and high scores
- Theme unlock progress
- User preferences (sound settings, selected theme)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. You can clear this data at any time through your device's app settings (Settings > Apps > Spot the Shade > Clear Data).

## Permissions

Spot the Shade does not request any sensitive permissions. The app requires no special device permissions to function.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Spot the Shade does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, no social features, and no data collection mechanisms. It is safe for use by children.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.
